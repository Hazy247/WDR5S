About WDR5

A plain-English overview for parents, relatives and professionals looking for a simple starting point.

What is WDR5-related neurodevelopmental disorder?

WDR5-related neurodevelopmental disorder is a very rare genetic condition linked to changes in the WDR5 gene. Because it is rare and recently described, many families find there is very little information available online.

This page should be treated as a family-friendly summary rather than medical advice. Families should always speak to their genetics or paediatric team about their own diagnosis.

Commonly discussed areas

Families may be looking for information about development, learning, autism, seizures, speech, movement, feeding, sleep and therapies. Each child is different.
